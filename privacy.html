Policy Overview

Last Updated: January 2025

CAPE Technology Solutions ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and share information in accordance with federal security requirements and defense industry standards.

Security Classification Notice: This privacy policy applies to unclassified information only. All classified information is handled under separate, classified security protocols.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, organization
  • Professional Information: Job title, security clearance level, professional interests
  • Communication Data: Messages, inquiries, and feedback submitted through our forms
  • Application Data: Resume, credentials, and career-related information for job applications

Information Collected Automatically

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system
  • Usage Analytics: Pages visited, time spent, navigation patterns (anonymized)
  • Security Logs: Access attempts, security events, and system monitoring data

How We Use Your Information

Communication & Support

Respond to inquiries, provide technical support, and maintain business relationships

Business Operations

Process partnership opportunities, evaluate project requirements, and manage contracts

Security & Compliance

Maintain system security, comply with federal regulations, and protect sensitive information

Service Improvement

Enhance our website, services, and user experience through analytics and feedback

Information Sharing Practices

We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Rent or lease your contact information
  • Share data for marketing purposes without consent
  • Disclose classified or sensitive information inappropriately

We May Share Information:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist with form processing and communications
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government investigation
  • Security Purposes: To protect our systems, investigate security incidents, or prevent fraud
  • Business Continuity: In connection with merger, acquisition, or transfer of business assets

Data Security Framework

Encryption & Access Controls
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest and in transit
  • Multi-factor authentication for system access
  • Role-based access controls and least privilege principles
Monitoring & Incident Response
  • 24/7 security monitoring and threat detection
  • Automated incident response procedures
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
Compliance & Auditing
  • FISMA Moderate compliance implementation
  • NIST 800-53 security controls
  • Regular compliance audits and assessments

While we implement industry-leading security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We continuously enhance our security posture to protect your information.
