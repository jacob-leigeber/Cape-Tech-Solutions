Who We Are

CAPE Technology Solutions is a team of creative, analytical, and practical professionals dedicated to delivering innovative solutions for complex challenges. Our diverse backgrounds and expertise allow us to approach problems from multiple perspectives, ensuring the best outcomes for our clients and partners.

CAPE Leadership

CAPE Leadership is comprised of 3x Army Veterans with a combined 70+ years of experience, both in and out of uniform, supporting Modeling and Simulation Software Development and Engineering. Their focus is on the people of CAPE by implementing work/life balance while maintaining a high level of proficiency. It’s the cornerstone to how the company is run: “Lead People; Manage Things.” Never confuse the two.
