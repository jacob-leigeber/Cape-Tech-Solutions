Defense Simulation

At CAPE Technology Solutions, we deliver cutting-edge modeling and simulation federations to empower U.S. and Coalition forces with realistic, operationally relevant training environments.

Joint Live Virtual and Constructive (JLVC) Federation

As the lead developer and integrator, we create the JLVC Federation, uniting live troops, virtual simulators, and constructive AI-driven forces into a seamless training environment. This enables joint forces to train across air, land, sea, space, and cyber domains with unparalleled realism.

Command Post Exercises (CPX)

Our simulation tools support CPX, enabling command staff to practice strategic decision-making and coordination in simulated battlefield scenarios, enhancing readiness without deploying physical forces.

Computer-Assisted Exercises (CAX)

We develop computer-based simulations for CAX, providing realistic platforms for military personnel to hone tactical and strategic skills in a controlled, software-driven environment.

Live, Virtual, and Constructive (LVC) Exercises

Our LVC solutions integrate real troops, virtual simulators, and automated constructive models, creating dynamic, multi-domain training exercises that mirror real-world operations.
